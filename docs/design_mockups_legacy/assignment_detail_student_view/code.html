Architectural History: The Renaissance Revival

DR. ELARA VANCE Posted Oct 12
100 points
Due Oct 24, 11:59 PM

For this week's Atelier assignment, you are tasked with analyzing the stylistic resurgence of Italian Renaissance principles in 19th-century public architecture. Focus your research on the balance between symmetry, proportion, and the decorative use of classical orders.

Please prepare a visual essay (approx. 1,500 words) that identifies at least three specific landmarks within the city that exhibit these characteristics. Your analysis should not only identify the elements but also explain the socio-political motivations behind choosing a Renaissance aesthetic for these structures.
